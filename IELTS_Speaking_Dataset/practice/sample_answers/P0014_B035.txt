Question: Describe a piece of technology you use regularly.
You should say:
• What it is
• How long you have been using it
• What you use it for
• And explain why it is important to you

You will have to speak about the topic for 1-2 minutes.
You have 1 minute to think about what you're going to say.
Band: 3.5
Topic: Technology

Sample Answer:
I would like to describe piece of technology you use regularly. This is something that I find particularly interesting and meaningful. Based on my personal experience, I have several points to share about this topic. First, let me tell you about my main impressions...

Keywords: about, like, describe, piece, technology, regularly, something, find, particularly, interesting
